RenewCanvas Africa

Transforming Plastic Waste into Sustainable Creative Value Across Africa

Presented by: Nicholas Eke | Founder

The Problem We're Solving

  • Over 400 million tonnes of plastic waste produced globally each year
  • Only 9% of plastic is recycled; the rest ends up in landfills or oceans
  • African cities face severe plastic pollution and inadequate waste management
  • Artists struggle to find markets, materials, and sustainable income
  • Consumers want sustainable products but lack trusted sources
